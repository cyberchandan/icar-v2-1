TENDER DOCUMENT

For

Migration, Redesign, Development and Maintenance of Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) website (icar.org.in)

1. Introduction

The Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR), an autonomous body under the Department of Agriculture and Education (DARE), Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare, Government of India wishes to redesign and develop a new website and migrate the contents from its old website. The new website is expected to follow GIGW 3.0 guidelines and the best practices including Digital Brand Identity Manual 3.0 (DBIM), ensuring full compliance with security standards and government requirements. The present document provides the technical requirements of the proposed website. The proposal is through open tender inviting from eligible, experienced, and qualified organizations to undertake the migration, redesign, development, and maintenance of the ICAR’s bilingual website. The ICAR also wishes to introduce a text-based AI-enabled chat bot help various type of stakeholders to navigate the website and get their queries resolved quickly. Another component included in this proposal is to develop a separate robust E-Commerce Platform to showcase and sell various types of products from constituent units of ICAR. It is also expected that the new website shall be cloud ready, deployable on a secured government cloud with necessary security audit certification for Government applications. The existing website is in Drupal 10 running in RHEL 9.5 with MYSQL 8.0 as the DBMS. The selected vendor will be responsible for the complete lifecycle of the website, including its development, migration from Drupal 10 to Drupal 11.3. (or latest stable version), deployment on a Government Cloud, as well as ensuring ongoing maintenance, security, and compliance for a period of initially for 1 year(after one year of warranty from Go Live date), and further extendable for 2 years+2 years based on certain conditions given in the financial bid.
